Frequently Asked Questions

Most of the questions and answers about licenses, orders and other issues that we have collected so far on ThemeOn.net

General

Please let us know via the contact page or email us at support[at]themeon.net; we'd appreciate any reporting of problems or bugs you might have found. We'll try to fix those problems or help you find a solution.
Don't hesitate to share your ideas and suggestions with us; we will consider them and try to implement them.

Licensing Questions

Yes, you can! Once you have purchased a theme, you can use it commercially.
Please visit our licenses page and read the full details on each type of license we offer. If you are still unsure about which to choose, please get in touch with us at support[at]themeon.net.
We love Open Source; please get in touch with us for details. Also, please provide a link to your project (such as Github, Gitlab, etc.) You may be able to get it for FREE.

Order Issues

Yes, you can; please visit the Redownload Theme page and fill out the form with the email address you used when purchasing. We will send you a new link shortly.
Due to technical difficulties, this may happen at times. Contact us, and we will resolve your issue as soon as possible.

If you accidentally placed an order and have not downloaded the theme, you can contact us to request a refund within three days of making the purchase.

On the other hand, we do not refund orders once the theme already been downloaded due to the nature of digital products.
